## Types of Disputes

### 1. Linguistic Disputes
Differences in interpreting a specific word without affecting the overall meaning.

### 2. Substantive Disputes
Differences in understanding the behavior itself or its ruling.

### 3. Application Disputes
Agreement on meaning with differences in application.
